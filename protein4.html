Vanilla Berry Protein Smoothie ngredients for 2 servings 1 ½ cups water (360 mL), or yogurt or milk of choice 1 scoop vanilla protein powder 1 cup strawberry (150 g), frozen 1 cup blueberry (100 g), frozen ½ cup raspberry (60 g), frozen ½ cup blackberry (75 g), frozen Preparation Put all ingredients into a blender and mix until smooth. Enjoy!
